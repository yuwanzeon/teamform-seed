    Administrator can add a member to a team. All teams this user applied will be shown on the user card as buttons. If you want to add this user to one of these teams, just simply click on the corresponding button. You could also enter the name of a team to add or drag the team card to corresponding user card.

  • import_exportMerge and Delete Teams

    To merge two teams, you could drag one team into another team, the information of dragged team will be added into the other one. You could also enter the name of the team to be merged into and click "MERGE". If you only want to delete a team, just simply click on "DELETE".
